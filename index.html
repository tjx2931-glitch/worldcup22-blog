Tournament Summary

The 2022 FIFA World Cup in Qatar was a tournament of firsts: the first in the Arab world, the most goals ever scored, and a final for the ages. Argentina, led by Lionel Messi, triumphed over France in a dramatic penalty shootout, while Morocco made history as the first African and Arab semi-finalist.
